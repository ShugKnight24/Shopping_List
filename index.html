The Premise

Shopping can be difficult, lists make things easier. Shopping + List = perhaps the greatest technological innovation since sliced bread!(Which you can ironically purchase thanks to this brilliant technological breakthrough!)

How it Works

I have created a form that you can use to enter the food you would like to purchase (from our predetermined list). You enter the prices for those items in your area, and my program will do all of the work. It will add up those items for you, create a list, and generate a total from our brilliant algorithms database!
