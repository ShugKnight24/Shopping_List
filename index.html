The Shopping List App

The Shopping List Vision

Are you the type of person who struggles in a grocery store? Does the pressure of standing in long aisles of food weight down on you? Have you ever walked in to buy something specfic and walked out with more items than you anticipated? Fear not! Statistics that probably don't exist and definitely cannot be verified say that you are not alone.

My Mission

Does that resonate with you? If it doesn't, you are just far, far superior to the rest of us and don't belong on this web app. If it does, I have dedicated my life to helping people like you. I myself hate grocery stores. Get me Amazon Fresh, and I'll be happy, but for now, these monolithic organizations persist, and we must go there for sustenance. Fear not! There is an app for that!

The Premise

Shopping can be difficult, lists make things easier. Shopping + List = perhaps the greatest technological innovation since sliced bread!(Which you can ironically purchase thanks to this brilliant technological breakthrough!). I created this app so that you can spend as little time as possible in those wretched grocery stores.

How it Works

There is a form below that you can use to enter the food and price of the item you would like to purchase, and my program will do all of the work. It will generate a list for you, add up the prices those items for you!
